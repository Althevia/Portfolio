Dance Party

June 28th, 2017

I learned that when wheels turn on an automobile, one wheel is going clockwise while the other is going counter-clockwise! It was a big shock because it seemed clear that since the wheels moved in the same direction (forward), that they'd turn in the same direction too.

My favorite part of my robot's dance was when it did drew a square while making dramatic spins on every corner
